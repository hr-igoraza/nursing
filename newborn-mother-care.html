Newborn & Mother Care

Welcoming a newborn is a joyous yet challenging experience for every mother. Ensuring the well-being of both the mother and baby during the postnatal period is crucial for a healthy recovery and smooth transition into parenthood. At Kochin LifeCare, we provide professional newborn and mother care services in Kaloor, Ernakulam ensuring comfort, safety, and expert medical assistance at home.

1. Postnatal Care for Mothers

  • Nutrition guidance and lactation support
  • Postpartum depression counseling
  • Personalized hygiene and wellness assistance
2. Newborn Baby Care
  • Expert guidance on newborn feeding and sleeping patterns
  • Baby massage and bath assistance
  • Monitoring baby’s health and development
  • Vaccination reminders and medical check-up coordination
3. Home-Based Nursing Support
  • 24/7 trained nursing support for mothers and newborns
  • Assistance with C-section recovery and wound care
  • Managing postnatal complications under medical supervision
